The Best Movies According to Lavanya

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Toy Story (1995):

Directed by John Lasseter, "Toy Story" was the first feature-length film created entirely with computer-generated animation. The heartwarming story of toys coming to life when their owners aren't around is a classic. It's known for its groundbreaking animation and endearing characters.

Directed by Andrew Stanton, "Finding Nemo" is a Pixar classic that follows the journey of Marlin, a clownfish, as he searches for his son, Nemo, who has been captured by a scuba diver. The film is celebrated for its stunning underwater visuals and emotional storytelling.
